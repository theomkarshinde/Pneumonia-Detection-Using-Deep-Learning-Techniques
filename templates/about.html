About Our Pneumonia Detection System

Using deep learning to assist in early pneumonia detection from X-ray images.

Our system utilizes state-of-the-art deep learning models to analyze chest X-rays and detect pneumonia with high accuracy. Our goal is to assist medical professionals in making faster and more reliable diagnoses.

We provide a platform where doctors can upload X-rays, receive AI-generated diagnoses, and track patient history seamlessly.
